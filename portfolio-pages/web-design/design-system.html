Engineering + Design

As someone who works in both design and engineering, building a design system is a way to create clarity, consistency, and efficiency across product development. I enjoy translating design principles into reusable code that's scalable, accessible, and easy to implement. Bridging both worlds allows me to anticipate needs, streamline collaboration, and ensure that design intent is carried through to the final user experience.

Types of Tokens

Prmitives

Context-agnostic and store raw values like hex codes for colors, or pixels or percentages for spacing and sizing, and more. They can also store information for typography, border radius, stroke width, spacing, sizing, animation, and more. For ease of brand theming it is helpful to add a layer of 'role' tokens to this tier
